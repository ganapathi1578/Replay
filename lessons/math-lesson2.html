Calculus Introduction

60 minutes Advanced Level Instructor: Dr. Johnson

About This Lesson

Welcome to the fascinating world of calculus! This lesson introduces you to the fundamental concepts of differential and integral calculus. You'll discover how calculus helps us understand rates of change and areas under curves, with applications spanning physics, engineering, economics, and beyond.

What You'll Learn:

  • The concept of limits and continuity
  • Introduction to derivatives and differentiation
  • Basic rules of differentiation
  • Introduction to integrals and integration
  • The fundamental theorem of calculus
  • Real-world applications of calculus

Prerequisites:

Strong foundation in algebra, trigonometry, and pre-calculus. Familiarity with functions and graphing is essential.

Additional Resources:

Access supplementary materials including formula sheets and practice problems.
