Electromagnetic Waves

55 minutes Advanced Level Instructor: Dr. Chen

About This Lesson

Dive into the fascinating world of electromagnetic radiation! Learn how electric and magnetic fields create waves that travel at the speed of light, encompassing everything from radio waves to gamma rays.

What You'll Learn:

  • The nature of electromagnetic waves
  • Wave properties: frequency, wavelength, and amplitude
  • The electromagnetic spectrum
  • Wave-particle duality
  • Applications in technology and medicine
  • Maxwell's equations (conceptual overview)

Prerequisites:

Understanding of electric and magnetic fields, wave mechanics, and basic trigonometry.

Additional Resources:

Spectrum diagrams and interactive wave simulations for enhanced learning.
