Basic Probability for Stochastic Calculus

17 minutes Beginner Level Instructor: Prof. Barun Sarkar

About This Lesson

This class introduces fundamental probability concepts essential for understanding stochastic processes. Before diving into stochastic calculus, it's important to build an intuitive and formal understanding of basic probability, random experiments, and outcomes.

What You'll Learn:

  • Sample spaces and events
  • Basic rules of probability
  • Discrete vs continuous probabilities
  • How these ideas connect to stochastic systems

Prerequisites:

Just high school-level math and a willingness to learn. No advanced knowledge required.
