Atomic Structure

40 minutes Beginner Level Instructor: Prof. Garcia

About This Lesson

Journey into the microscopic world of atoms! Discover the fundamental building blocks of matter and learn how protons, neutrons, and electrons combine to create all the elements we know.

What You'll Learn:

  • The structure of atoms: nucleus and electron shells
  • Properties of protons, neutrons, and electrons
  • Atomic number and mass number
  • Isotopes and their applications
  • Electron configuration and orbital theory
  • The periodic table organization

Prerequisites:

Basic mathematics and introduction to scientific notation. No prior chemistry knowledge required.

Additional Resources:

Interactive periodic table and atomic model animations.
