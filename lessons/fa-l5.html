Class 5: Hölder's Inequality, Minkowski's Inequality & Related Proofs

55 minutes Intermediate Level Instructor: Prof. Raghavendra

About This Lesson

In this lecture, Prof. Raghavendra provides a comprehensive exploration of key inequalities in normed vector spaces. The session covers Hölder’s inequality, Minkowski’s inequality, and related results, with rigorous proofs and applications to $L^p$ spaces.

What You'll Learn:

  • Statement and proof of Hölder’s inequality
  • Statement and proof of Minkowski’s inequality
  • Applications of these inequalities to $L^p$ and normed spaces
  • Connections to triangle inequality and inner product spaces
  • Common problem-solving strategies using these inequalities

Prerequisites:

Knowledge of normed spaces, inner product spaces, and basic real analysis concepts from previous classes is recommended.
