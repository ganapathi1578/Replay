Chemical Bonding

48 minutes Intermediate Level Instructor: Dr. Thompson

About This Lesson

Understand how atoms combine to form molecules and compounds! Learn about the different types of chemical bonds and how they determine the properties of substances around us.

What You'll Learn:

  • Ionic bonding and formation of ionic compounds
  • Covalent bonding and molecular structures
  • Metallic bonding and properties of metals
  • Lewis structures and electron dot diagrams
  • Predicting bond types and molecular shapes
  • Intermolecular forces and their effects

Prerequisites:

Understanding of atomic structure, electron configuration, and basic periodic table trends.

Additional Resources:

Molecular modeling tools and bonding practice worksheets.
