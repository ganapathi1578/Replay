Introduction to Neural Networks

35 minutes Beginner Level Instructor: Dr. D Satyaprasad

About This Lesson

Dr. D Satyaprasad introduces the fundamentals of neural networks, explaining their structure, basic working principles, and significance in modern artificial intelligence applications.

What You'll Learn:

  • Basic architecture of a neural network
  • How neurons process and transmit information
  • Overview of activation functions and learning process
  • Applications of neural networks in real-world AI

Prerequisites:

Basic knowledge of linear algebra and probability is recommended but not required.
