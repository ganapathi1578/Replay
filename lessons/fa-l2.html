Metric & Normed Spaces

52 minutes Beginner Level Instructor: Prof. Raghavendra

About This Lesson

In this second lecture of Functional Analysis, Prof. Raghavendra introduces two essential structures in mathematical analysis: metric spaces and normed vector spaces. These foundational concepts are vital for understanding continuity, convergence, and structure in infinite-dimensional spaces.

What You'll Learn:

  • Definition and examples of metric spaces
  • Open and closed sets, convergence, and completeness
  • Introduction to normed vector spaces
  • Relation between norm and metric
  • Examples like \( \ell^p \), \( C[a, b] \), and more

Prerequisites:

Basic understanding of set theory, functions, and real analysis is helpful.
