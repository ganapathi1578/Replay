Stochastic Calculus – Class 5: Random Variables & Measurability

45 minutes Beginner Level Instructor: Prof. Barun Sarkar

About This Lesson

Prof. Barun Sarkar introduces the concept of random variables as measurable functions, explains measurability in probability theory, and connects these ideas to the foundations of stochastic processes.

What You'll Learn:

  • Definition and interpretation of random variables
  • Measurable functions in probability spaces
  • Examples of measurable and non-measurable sets
  • Importance in stochastic modeling

Prerequisites:

Completion of Class 4 on probability continuation is recommended.
