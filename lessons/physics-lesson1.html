Newton's Laws of Motion

50 minutes Intermediate Level Instructor: Prof. Wilson

About This Lesson

Explore the three fundamental laws that revolutionized our understanding of motion and forces. Newton's laws form the foundation of classical mechanics and explain everything from why objects fall to how rockets reach space.

What You'll Learn:

  • Newton's First Law: The Law of Inertia
  • Newton's Second Law: F = ma
  • Newton's Third Law: Action and Reaction
  • Free body diagrams and force analysis
  • Applications in everyday phenomena
  • Problem-solving techniques

Prerequisites:

Basic understanding of vectors, algebra, and familiarity with concepts of force and motion.

Additional Resources:

Interactive simulations and practice problems to test your understanding.
