Introduction to Fluid Dynamics

30 minutes Beginner Level Instructor: Prof. S. R. Manam

About This Lesson

Welcome to the Fluid Dynamics course! In this introductory lesson, we’ll explore what fluids are, get a broad overview of fluid flow fundamentals, understand the scope of fluid dynamics, and learn where it's used — especially in computer simulations. We’ll also touch on the basics of Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD).

What You'll Learn:

  • What fluids are and how they behave
  • The purpose and scope of fluid dynamics
  • Where fluid dynamics is used (engineering, simulations, etc.)
  • A first look at Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)

Prerequisites:

No prior knowledge required — just curiosity and interest in real-world physical systems.

Additional Resources:

Slides and references will be shared in upcoming sessions as we go deeper into the subject.
