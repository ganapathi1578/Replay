Class 3: Cauchy-Schwarz Inequality & Rigorous Proofs

47 minutes Intermediate Level Instructor: Prof. Raghavendra

About This Lesson

Prof. Raghavendra walks through one of the most fundamental inequalities in analysis — the Cauchy-Schwarz inequality — and offers rigorous step-by-step proofs with real-world intuition.

What You'll Learn:

  • Statement and geometric meaning of the Cauchy-Schwarz Inequality
  • Proofs using inner product spaces
  • Applications in normed vector spaces
  • Equality conditions and examples

Prerequisites:

Completion of Class 2 (Metric and Normed Spaces) is recommended for context.
