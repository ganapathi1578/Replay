Fluid Dynamics – Class 2: What Are Fluids?

45 minutes Beginner Level Instructor: Prof. S. R. Manam

About This Lesson

In this second session, we go deeper into understanding fluids — what defines a fluid, their fundamental properties, and how we approach studying fluid motion through theoretical, experimental, and computational techniques.

What You'll Learn:

  • Definition of a fluid and its key characteristics
  • Essential properties like viscosity, density, and pressure
  • Theoretical analysis of fluid motion
  • Experimental methods used in fluid studies
  • Introduction to Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) techniques

Prerequisites:

Basic understanding of the introduction session is recommended but not required.
