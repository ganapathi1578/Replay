Stochastic Calculus – Basics

45 minutes Beginner Level Instructor: Prof. Barun Sarkar

About This Lesson

This introductory session on stochastic calculus covers essential foundational topics that are crucial for understanding randomness in mathematical systems. Perfect for beginners, this 45-minute lecture sets the stage for more advanced topics.

What You'll Learn:

  • Definition and importance of countable and uncountable sets
  • Introduction to sigma-fields
  • Core concepts in probability theory relevant to stochastic processes
  • Mathematical structures underpinning random variables

Prerequisites:

A basic understanding of set theory and probability concepts is helpful but not required.
