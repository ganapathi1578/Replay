Stochastic Calculus – Class 3 Day 3: Probability Continuation

40 minutes Beginner Level Instructor: Prof. Barun Sarkar

About This Lesson

In this continuation of probability foundations, Prof. Barun Sarkar builds further on events and probability spaces, exploring how sigma fields function and set up the stage for defining measurable events in stochastic processes.

What You'll Learn:

  • Refinement of probability axioms
  • Event algebra and sigma fields revisited
  • Intuitive understanding of measurable sets
  • Relevance of these ideas in stochastic modeling

Prerequisites:

Completion of Class 2 on sets and basic probability is recommended.
